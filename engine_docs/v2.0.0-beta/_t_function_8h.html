class  ph::function_detail::TFunction< R(Args...), MIN_SIZE_HINT >
 Lightweight callable target wrapper. This type is a thin wrapper around stateless callable targets such as free function, method, functor and lambda. For methods, the instance must outlive the associated TFunction. For functors and lambdas, they must be stateless (no member variable/no capture) or small enough in size. Unlike the standard std::function which offers little guarantees, this type is guaranteed to be cheap to construct, copy, destruct and small in size (minimum: 2 pointers for MIN_SIZE_HINT == 16, may vary depending on the platform).
